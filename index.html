Clix Live Better
Clix is a mobile app that will be able to suggest personalized itineraries and activities based on your interests, money-on-hand, and habits. Allowing you to experience the best of what the city has to offer on any budget.
PLACELIST TECHNOLOGY

Users will be able to share these experienced iteneraries both long and short as a Placelist™. Users on our social network will be able to: See Placelists™ from friends, like-minded individuals, celebrities, and more. Upvote and Downvote suggested Placelists™ so that recommendations become better after every use. Plan optimal itineraries and activites for a group of friends with our group recommendation feature.
